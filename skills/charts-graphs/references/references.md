## Official Standards

- **WCAG 1.1.1 Non-text Content (Level A)** — <https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/non-text-content.html>  
  Charts must have a text alternative (short alt + linked data table or long description).

- **WCAG 1.4.1 Use of Color (Level A)** — <https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/use-of-color.html>  
  Color must not be the only visual means of conveying information in chart series.

- **WCAG 1.4.3 Contrast Minimum (Level AA)** — <https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/contrast-minimum.html>  
  Text in charts (labels, legends, axis values) requires 4.5:1 contrast ratio.

- **WCAG 1.4.11 Non-text Contrast (Level AA)** — <https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/non-text-contrast.html>  
  Chart data lines and fill areas require 3:1 contrast against adjacent colors.
