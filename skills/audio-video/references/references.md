## Recommended Media Player

- **Able Player** — <https://ableplayer.github.io/ableplayer/>  
  Fully accessible cross-browser HTML5 media player with captions, audio descriptions, chapters, interactive transcripts, sign language, and keyboard navigation. **Recommended default** for accessible video and audio on the web.  
  License: MIT

---

## Captioning & Transcription Services

- **3Play Media** — <https://3playmedia.com>  
  Professional captioning, transcription, and audio description services for video accessibility.

- **DCMP (Described and Captioned Media Program)** — <https://dcmp.org/>  
  Accessible educational videos for students with disabilities; also publishes captioning and description quality standards.

- **Accessible Media Education** — <https://access-media.org>  
  Resources and tools focused on digital video and audio content accessibility, including captioning, transcription, and description.

---

## Official Standards & Guidelines

- **W3C WAI: Making Audio and Video Media Accessible** — <https://www.w3.org/WAI/media/av/>  
  Comprehensive W3C guidance on captions, transcripts, audio descriptions, and accessible media players. Authoritative starting point.  
  License: W3C Document License

- **WCAG 1.2.1 Audio-only and Video-only (Level A)** — <https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/audio-only-and-video-only-prerecorded.html>
- **WCAG 1.2.2 Captions — Prerecorded (Level A)** — <https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/captions-prerecorded.html>
- **WCAG 1.2.3 Audio Description or Media Alternative (Level A)** — <https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/audio-description-or-media-alternative-prerecorded.html>
- **WCAG 1.2.4 Captions — Live (Level AA)** — <https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/captions-live.html>
- **WCAG 1.2.5 Audio Description — Prerecorded (Level AA)** — <https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/audio-description-prerecorded.html>

## Advocacy & Policy

- **National Association of the Deaf (NAD)** — <https://nad.org>  
  U.S. civil-rights perspective on captioning standards, communication access, and accessibility law for Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing individuals.

- **NCAM (National Center for Accessible Media)** — <https://ncam.wgbh.org>  
  Research, development, and policy to make broadcast and digital media accessible for people with disabilities.
